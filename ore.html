How does iron ore look like and where do we get it?

Iron ore is some kind of iron compound casually found in the for of oxides such as Fe₃O₄ - magnetite, Fe₂O₃ and etc. It also found as goethite - FeO(OH), limonite FeO(OH)·n(H₂O), siderite FeCO₃

Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan territory contains approximately 18.6 billion tonnes of iron ore and is the 6th by the reserve volume and 7th by quality(ratio of iron in ore) in the world. There are numerous iron mines and the largest are(2023 data):

  1. Sokolovsky Mine - 7.52 mtpa
  2. Kacharsky Mine - 6.84 mtpa
  3. Sarbaisky Iron Ore Mine - 6.49 mtpa
  4. Korzhinkolskoye Mine - 2.02 mtpa
  5. Atasu Mine - 1.29 mtpa
